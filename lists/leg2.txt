Lancair Legacy (Aerobask/X-Plane)
--Before Start--
Wght & Bal, Oxy, Maint=Chkd
Docs/Charts/NOTAMS=Onboard
Prkg Brk=Set
Walkaround=Complete
Batt/Alt/Avionics=Off
L/G=Dwn
Nav Lht=On
GPU=As req
--Starting--
Batt Chrgd=Batt on, GPU/Alt Air off
Fuel=Check, L/R
Pwr=Mix rich, prop full, throttle 1/4"
Pump=Check, prime as req
Prop Clear=Mags on, start egn
Alt/Elec Trim/Avionics/Radios=On
Pwr=Idle throttle (>800rpm), lean mix
--Pre-Taxi--
Sys Tsts=AOA/ELT
Flt Plan/Altimeters/Radios/Xpdr=Set
Canopy=Latched, door seal on
Hdg=Rwy
Flaps/Trims=Set
Instr=Check
Cntrls=Free/Correct
--Taxi--
Xpdr, Taxi Lht=On
Brks=Chkd
--Run Up--
Taxi Lht=Off
Prkg Brk=Set
Oil Temp & CHT=100f/38c & 150f/65c min
Pwr=Mix rich, throttle 1800rpm
Prop=Cycle twice
Mags=Check, max 150 drop & 50 diff
Pwr=Throttle check full out then idle, lean mix
**Taxi Lhts as req leaving runup
--Before Takeoff--
Hdg=Set init crs
Instr=Chkd
AP/FD=Off/As desired
--Takeoff--
Strobes/Lndg=On
Xpdr=Alt
Pwr=Mix rich, prop full
Pump=As req
--Climb--
Clr Rwy=L/G up
800'=Flaps up, taxi/lndg/strobes & pump off
Pwr=2500rpm, lean mix, autotrack as req
**Oil Temp & CHT=240f/116c & 500f/260c max
Altimeters=Set thru clb
Fuel=Balanced
10,000' day/5,000' night=Oxy on
--Cruise--
Pwr/Autotrack=As desired
**CHT=Blw 435f/224c max cruise, 400f/205c econ cruise
Instr/Nav/Fuel/Altimeters=Monitor & set as req
--Descent--
Fuel=Balanced
Altimeters=Set
Pwr/Autotrack=As req
**CHT >180f/82c
10,000' day/5,000' night=Oxy off
--Pre Landing--
Fuel=Fullest tank
Pwr=Mix rich, prop high
L/G=Dwn, 3 green
**140kts max
Flaps=Set
Landing/Taxi/Strobes=On
Spdbrks=On after touchdown
--Taxi In--
Pwr=Throttle idle, lean mix
Flaps=Up
Trims=Neutral
Strobes/Lndg, Elec Trim, Door Seal=Off
Xpdr=On
--Shutdown--
Xpdr/Radios/Avionics=Off
Mags=Check, max 150 drop & 50 diff
Pwr=Throttle/Mix cutoff
Fuel/Mags/Lhts/Alt/Batt=Off
Covers/Chocks/Canopy Latch=Secure
