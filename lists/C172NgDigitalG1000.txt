**EXEMPLE OF ROUTE:
**NTAA/04 N0433F260 VAIT4E VAITE DCT HHN RU1B NTTB/11
**SEQUENCE FOR ENTERING FPL:
**01-PRESS FPL BUTTON
**02-PRESS FMS BUTTON (THE DEPARTURE AIRPORT SHOULD BE HIGHLIGHTED)
**03-PRESS PROC BUTTON
**04-SELECT "SELECT DEPARTURE" THEN PRESS ENT BUTTON
**05-SELECT "VAIT4E" THEN PRESS ENT BUTTON TWO TIMES
**06-SELECT "THE EMPTY SPACE" AFTER VAITE
**07-ENTER "HHN" THEN PRESS ENT UNTIL SEE FPL
**08-ENTER "NTTB" AS ARRIVAL, THEN PRESS ENT UNTIL SEE FPL
**09-SELECT "NTTB" (THE ARRIVAL AIRPORT SHOULD BE HIGHLIGHTED)
**10-PRESS PROC BUTTON
**11-SELECT "SELECT ARRIVAL" THEN PRESS ENT BUTTON
**12-SELECT "RUB1" THEN PRESS ENT UNTIL SEE MAP
**13-PRESS PROC BUTTON
**14-SELECT "SELECT APPROACH" THEN PRESS ENT
**15-SELECT "RNP11-Y" THEN PRESS ENT
**16-SELECT "BAMBI" AS VECTORS THEN PRESS ENT
**17-SELECT MINIMUM AND SET "BARO" THEN FEET VALUE THEN PRESS ENT UNTIL SEE MAP
**IMPORTANT:
**THE LAST WAYPOINT OF THE ARRIVAL SHOULD BE THE FIRST WAYPOINT OF THE APPROACH

**YOU MUST DELETE OR ADD SOME WAYPOINT AFTER THAT
**1-SELECT "BB" THEN ENTER BAMBI THEN PRESS ENT
**2-SELECT "BB" JUST BEFORE THE APPROACH SECTION THEN PRESS CLR
**3-SELECT THEN DELETE THE "NTTB" JUST AFTER THE FIRST HHN POINT USING CLR BUTTON
**IMPORTANT:
**THE "NTTB" MUST BE NOT HERE IN THE FPL (JUST "NTAA")
